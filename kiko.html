What are smart devices?

Smart devices are electronic gadgets that can connect to the internet or other devices to perform automated tasks, enhance convenience, and provide various functionalities, often controlled remotely via a smartphone or computer. Examples include smart speakers, thermostats, lights, and home security systems.

Examples of Smart Devices

Explore these innovative devices that bring convenience and automation to your everyday life

Google Nest Hub

Acts as a control center for your smart home, displaying visual responses, managing connected devices, playing music, and providing daily summaries using voice commands.

Samsung Family Hub Refrigerator
